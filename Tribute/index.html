✨ Why I Admire Him

Albert Einstein was a theoretical physicist who developed the theory of relativity—one of the two pillars of modern physics. He is best known for his equation E = mc².

He wasn’t just a scientist—he was a dreamer, a thinker, and a humanitarian. He spoke out for civil rights, world peace, and education.

His creativity and courage to challenge existing beliefs is what makes him truly admirable. He proved that imagination is the root of innovation.

“Imagination is more important than knowledge.”
